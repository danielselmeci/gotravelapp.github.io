Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Introduction

At GoTravelApp, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Create an account
  • Add travel documents
  • Set up trip itineraries and agenda items
  • Track travel expenses
  • Use interactive maps and location services
  • Contact our support team

Travel Documents

We collect information from your travel documents, including:

  • Passport information
  • Visa details
  • Travel dates and trip itineraries
  • Document expiration dates
  • Expense records and budget information

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our services
  • Send you notifications about document expirations and trip reminders
  • Help you track and manage your travel expenses
  • Provide interactive maps and location-based services
  • Improve our app's functionality and user experience
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Send you important updates about our services

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information:

  • All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • We use industry-standard security practices
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to personal information

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We use cloud storage providers that comply with international data protection standards.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Export your data

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services that collect, monitor, and analyze data. These services include:

  • Analytics providers
  • Cloud storage services
  • Payment processors

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • By email: privacy@gotravelapp.com
  • Through our support page: Support
