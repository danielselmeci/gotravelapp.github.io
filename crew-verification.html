Crew Verification

Why we verify crew members (and how to get approved fast).

Why verification matters

Layover Lounge chats contain flight-sensitive information: routes, hotel assignments, transportation, and local safety tips. Verification lets us confirm that every participant actually works for the airline operating the layover, keeping conversations private and trusted.

Instant verification via airline email

  1. Open GoTravelApp → Crew+ → Tap “Get verified instantly”.
  2. Enter your airline-issued email address and tap “Send link”.
  3. Confirm the one-time link sent to that inbox. That’s it—approval is instant once the link is opened.

Tip: the link expires after 15 minutes—request a new one if it times out.

No airline email? Upload documents instead

You can still get verified—approval usually takes less than 24 hours.

  • Open GoTravelApp → Crew+ → “Upload crew ID or employment letter”.
  • Choose one of the following:
    • Current crew ID (front + back)
    • Recent employment letter on airline letterhead
    • Paystub dated within the last 60 days
  • We’ll email you when the document is approved—or if we need a clearer copy.

Keeping your information private

  • Uploads are encrypted at rest and permanently deleted once reviewed.
  • Only our small verification team can view submissions.
  • Verification data is never shared with other crew members or third parties.

Join the layover chat after approval

  1. Once approved, tap any layover invite QR code (or re-scan the one you already have).
  2. The app will add you to the chat automatically if you’re rostered on that airline.
  3. If the lounge requires host approval, we’ll send the request for you.

Need help?

Reach our verification team directly:

  • Email: verification@gotravelapp.com
  • In-app: Crew+ → “Need help getting verified?”

We respond within 12 hours (often much sooner).
